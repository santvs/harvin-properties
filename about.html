✦ Our Story

Built by People Who Get It

Harvin Properties was founded by a licensed real estate agent who experienced first-hand how broken, expensive, and opaque the rental process had become — and decided to fix it.

Our Story

From Frustration
to Foundation

Harvin Properties was born out of a simple frustration: property management was designed for institutions, not people. Landlords were paying double-digit commissions for opaque service. Tenants were chasing agents for basic updates. Managers were drowning in paperwork.

Vinutha Narayan, a seasoned RE/MAX agent with deep roots in the Pacific Northwest, saw this every day. Together with a co-founding team of technologists and property professionals, she set out to build something better — a platform that puts transparency, technology, and trust at the center of every lease.

Harvin Properties is that platform. Simpler. Cheaper. Kinder to everyone involved.

🏡

Our Mission

Making Every Lease

We believe the rental market works better for everyone when information is clear, payments are automatic, and communication is seamless. That's what we build toward every day.

From the Pacific Northwest and growing — Harvin Properties is your home, our priority.
